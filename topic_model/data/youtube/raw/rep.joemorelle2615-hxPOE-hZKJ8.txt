today i rise to recognize extraordinary and impactful life of gloria langston community advocate and co-founder of rochester's first black-owned radio station who sadly passed away last week founded in 1974 wdkx was one of the first black owned stations in new york state their call sign is a tribute to frederick douglass martin luther king jr and malcolm x demonstrating their commitment to uplifting the black community together with her husband andrew langston gloria established wdkx as a fixture in the rochester radio space bringing people together and keeping our community engaged my heart goes out to her beloved son andre and the entire langston family during this difficult time may you find peace in knowing her legacy will live on through the good works of those she has inspired thank you mr speaker
