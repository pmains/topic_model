Georgia Senator Jon Ossoff stopped by Troup County with a major announcement today. Senator Ossoff visited The Ray--an 18 mile stretch of highway dedicated to clean energy--as well as Kia Georgia in West Point. Today Ossoff unveiled his proposal for the Sustainable Highways Innovation Act. He said the legislation will permit the use of underutilized stretches of highway for clean energy infrastructure. He also highlighted his push with Senator Raphael Warnock to pass a bill boosting American car manufacturing. <"The shortage of semiconductors is a major issue for auto manufacturers including KIA and for a wide range of American industries.
