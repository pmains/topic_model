U.S senators Catherine Cortez masto and Jackie Rosen have announced over two million dollars in funding from the bipartisan infrastructure law that will go toward cleanup Legacy pollution sites in Nevada the department of the Interior funding will be doled out to two projects one in Lincoln County for the Castleton mine and Mill and impacted watersheds restoration project and will focus on three washes impacted by mining in Castleton the second project in Humboldt County is for the riparian fencing project to repair or reconstruct around 40 miles of riparian fencing as part of a larger effort to restore degraded streams critical to the lahon cutthroat trout
