U-S Senator Jon Ossoff visited the Kia Plant in Georgia to talk about the infrastructure bill. the plan aims to prevent future shortages by boosting domestic semi- conductor manufacturing. Senators Ossoff and Warnock helped pass the legislation. KIA thanked them for working to provide all U-S manufacturers access to these important components in the future. "When an auto manufacture in Georgia faced a shortage of critical components Georgia senators reacted together quickly into action to expedite passage of legislation quickly to solve the problem. We are in the business of solving the problems for the people of Georgia and that legislation was passed on an expedited we are working hard to ensure to that it will pass the house and go to the president for a signature." The Senate also passed the bipartisan U-S Innovation and Competition Act to make historic investments in American innovation and scientific research and development  including 52 billion dollars to boost domestic semiconductor manufacturing.
