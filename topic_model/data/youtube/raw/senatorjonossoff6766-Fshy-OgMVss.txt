New at 11, United States Senator Jon Ossoff says he will sponsor proposed legislation to end pay discrimination against black women. Senator Ossoff joined Albany State University's President, and Albany Community leaders at Albany State university tonight for a round table discussion on Pay Equity for Black Women. The Equal Pay for Equal Work Enforcement Act, which he introduced alongside Georgia Congresswoman Nikema Williams, aims to end pay discrimination against Black women and finally guarantee equal pay for equal work.
