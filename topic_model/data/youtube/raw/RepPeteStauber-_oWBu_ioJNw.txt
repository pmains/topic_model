Bruce tender for his exceptional contributions to the state of Minnesota as an educator businessman and community leader from 1975 to 1981 Bruce worked as the first lay president of the College of st. Scholastica will publicly honor Bruce's illustrious career through the naming of the Stender School of Business and Technology the naming of this department after Bruce will ensure generations to come will remember his service and generosity I am happy to see Bruce receive this recognition and cannot think of a better way to celebrate his life's work Mr Speaker I yield back
