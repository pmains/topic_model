mr speaker i rise today to recognize march 2017 as national colorectal cancer awareness month this month offers us an opportunity to raise awareness about colorectal cancer and to recommit to taking action against this disease colorectal cancer is one of the most is one of the most preventable forms of cancer yet it remains the second leading cause of cancer death among men and women combined in the united states this year more than 130 000 individuals in the united states will be diagnosed with colorectal cancer approximately 50 000 more will die from it too often individuals are forced to forego screening because of high insurance costs in order to get more people screened and save lives we need to break down the financial barriers to treatment last month i joined with congressman charlie dent and leonard lance to introduce the removing barriers to colorectal cancer screening act our bill eliminates colonoscopy cost sharing for medicaid medicare patients so that every patient has access to this life-saving treatment mr speaker we cannot let costs stand in the way of care i urge congress to quickly advance this legislation patients are counting on it and i yield back
