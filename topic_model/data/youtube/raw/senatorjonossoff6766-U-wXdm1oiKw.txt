Georgia Senator Jon Ossoff has called on the Department of Health and Human Services to allocate COVID Relief funds for rural hospitals and clinics In a letter Ossoff is calling for swift action to provide support to these hospitals and clinics. The letter said quote "I write today to urge the immediate provision of Provider Relief Funds to rural clinics and hospitals and other rural health care providers in Georgia and nationwide." The letter goes on to state the funds promised back in May still have not been allocated quote "The American Rescue Plan authorized 8.5 billion dollars for rural health care providers to address expenses and lost revenue attributable to COVID-19."
