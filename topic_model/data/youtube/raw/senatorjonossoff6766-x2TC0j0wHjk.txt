A BIPARTISAN BILL  INTRODUCED BY GEORGIA  SENATOR JON OSSOFF TO  REFORM THE USE OF PRISON  CAMERAS--JUST PASSED THE  U-S SENATE.      THE LEGISLATION WILL  REQUIRE THE FEDERAL BUREAU  OF PRISONS TO MAKE SURE ALL  PRISONS HAVE THE CAMERA  COVERAGE NECESSARY---TO  PROTECT THE CIVIL RIGHTS  AND SAFETY OF INCARCERATED  PEOPLE AND STAFF.
